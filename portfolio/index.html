About

Builder01

accepting requests

The short version

Builder01 is the designation Jared ships under. Yes, the Really Exhausted Dude from the front door. This dude works with you end to end: the listening, the designing, the shipping, and the unglamorous stretch after shipping where it has to actually work. You arrive with the problem that is quietly eating your week. You leave without it.

What gets built

  • Custom tools for operations that have outgrown their spreadsheets: portals, workflows, dashboards, the quiet plumbing that holds a programme together.
  • The range runs from a small tool shipped in days to multi-role platforms run against national deadlines. Straightforward problems stay straightforward; complicated ones get made to look it.
  • AI where it earns its keep: agents with real governance, synthesis that cites its sources. Never a chatbot bolted on for show.

How it gets built

  • Simplicity over cleverness. If a solution feels complicated, it is the wrong one.
  • Humans keep the wheel. The software surfaces, suggests and logs; a person decides. Nothing consequential happens on autopilot.
  • Small does not mean rough. A tool for 10 people gets the same finish as one for thousands and beyond.

The evidence

Talk is cheap; deployments are on file. Two redacted projects, one live app, and one classified build in the lab. Inspect them in the projects index.
